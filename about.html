The Story

Excuse to Football started in Deal, Kent—a seaside town better known for retirement than revolution. I'm Martin: postal worker by day (23 years, 13-16 miles walking daily), scout and creator by night.

The first cards sold in local galleries to fathers buying for sons. That told the story: this wasn't about memorabilia. It was about passing down an eye for talent.

One of Our Own (4xO)

A limited-edition physical card with an embedded NFC chip. Tap it, and you unlock a dual-mode AI agent:

The Vault

For adults, collectors, enthusiasts. Elite tactical analysis, performance stats, career projections, scout-level insights.

The Sidekick

For young players (7-14). Fun coaching games, skill development drills, "Sidekick Way" methodology. Age-appropriate football intelligence.

Sidekick Way

The coaching philosophy baked into every card. It's Musashi's five rings meets 4v4 small-sided games.

We develop support players over heroes. Because football is a team sport, and the best players make everyone around them better.

Why This Exists

Most football content is either hyper-analytical (stats, xG, heat maps) or pure entertainment (highlights, banter). Nothing sits in the middle: accessible intelligence.

These cards are that middle ground. Physical art you can hold, digital intelligence you can access. A way to say: "I spotted this player before everyone else did."

The Mechanics

  • • Original halftone artwork — created from football timelapses (TikTok/Instagram/YouTube)
  • • NFC chip — embedded in graded slab case, links to Poe AI agent
  • • Limited batches — 25-50 units per player, pre-orders only
  • • Live updates — agent evolves as player's career develops
  • • Serial authentication — each card is unique, verifiable
